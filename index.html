Hi there!

I am a 22-year old UI/UX designer at UIUX-lingo, a startup dedicated to educating students the ins and outs of User Interface and Experience Design.

I recently graduated from Brown University with a B.S. in Compuer Science on the design track.

I am passionate about the process of designing applications that provide the best experience for its users. Sharing these insights with others helps spread best practices and allows us to make an impact on our future UI/UX designers!

Background

Growing up, I had my older sister, Miley, to guide me through life! Whether it comes to school, relationships, or learning how to cook, my sister is there for me every step of the way. She learned many things the hard way and ensured that I would not follow in those same footsteps.
